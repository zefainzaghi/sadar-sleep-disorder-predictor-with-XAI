Understand Your Sleep Disorders

The three most common conditions that can be detected from your sleep patterns.

01 — Most Common

Insomnia

Difficulty falling or staying asleep, despite having adequate opportunity and conditions for sleep.

Hard to fall asleep Waking up too early Daytime fatigue
02 — Respiratory

Sleep Apnea

Breathing repeatedly stops and starts during sleep, often unnoticed by the sufferer.

Loud snoring Shortness of breath upon waking Excessive sleepiness
03 — Normal Sleep

Healthy Pattern

Sleeping 7–9 hours with good quality and waking up refreshed is a sign of a healthy sleep pattern.

Easy to fall asleep Rarely wake up Wake up refreshed
